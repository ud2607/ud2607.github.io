Data Collection

In this project we collected data on our use of social media. Everday, we recorded the amount of time we used social media and certain apps on our phones. Personally, I recorded how many minutes I spent liking posts on Instagram, scrolling through memes on Instagram, and how many minutes of my day I spent watching dance videos. This project was a 2 week project that showed us how much of we used social media or other certain apps such as spotify. During this project, I recorded the amount of time I spent on this app and tried to record how much time I spent on my top four different activities on Instagram.
